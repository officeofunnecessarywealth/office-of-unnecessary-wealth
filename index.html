The Public Grant for Unnecessary Wealth

Applications are now open for proposals that generate prestige, excess, symbolic value, or other non-essential forms of wealth without serving any necessary practical function.

Submit Proposal Read Guidelines

The Office of Unnecessary Wealth supports projects that are refined, ambitious, and fundamentally unnecessary. We are interested in objects, systems, services, ceremonies, memberships, platforms, monuments, archives, and other structures capable of producing irrational desirability, ornamental authority, emotional luxury, or disproportionate prestige.

Submissions close on May 31, 2026
